Configura i temi della mappa¶

The beautiful thing about GIS is that maps are dynamic. Layers can individually be shown and hidden and information can be presented more or less prominently based on the task at hand.

This is what Map Themes (before QGIS 3.0 known as Visibility Presets) are for.

Creazione del tema della mappa¶

Creare un tema da una mappa in QGIS è una operazione molto semplice.

  1. Stilizza la mappa e i layers secondo le tue necessità
  2. Salvalo come una tema di mappa
  3. Load the project on your device and change the active Map Theme

Change the active Map Theme¶

Switching Map Themes is possible from the Dashboard.

Use the Map Themes combobox to chose the active theme.
